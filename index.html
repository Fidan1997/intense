Kids Are The Best Explorers In The World

Since 2001, Intense has been providing top-quality, AMI Certified Montessori Education to Elementary, Primary, and Toddler aged children in the area.

Welcome to Intense


At Intense, our mission is to provide excellence in preschool, kindergarten, before and after school programs and summer camp for families living in the greater local area. Here you’ll find exceptional teachers, curriculum and learning environments that encourage children to learn, play and explore in a safe and nurturing environment.
